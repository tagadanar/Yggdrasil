# NEWS SERVICE COMPREHENSIVE REVIEW AND FIXING PLAN

## OVERVIEW
The news service is experiencing critical performance issues (10-second response times) and functional problems (articles not appearing in feed despite successful creation). This document outlines a comprehensive review and fixing plan.

## CRITICAL ISSUES IDENTIFIED
1. **Performance Issues**: 10-second response times indicate MongoDB connection problems with 10s timeout fallbacks
2. **Data Persistence**: Articles created but not appearing in news feed suggests data layer issues
3. **MongoDB Integration**: Service repeatedly falling back to in-memory storage due to connection timeouts
4. **Test Coverage**: Integration tests have TypeScript errors and MongoDB authentication issues
